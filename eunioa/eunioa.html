EUNOIA

Eunioa was founded in November(2023), is a creative platform that transforms fashion into a powerful language of storytelling. We believe that every garment, every accessory and every style tells a story- of a person, a place or an idea.

About Eunioa

  1. Creative Storytelling: Eunoia weaves narratives into our designs, using fashion to express diverse cultural stories.
  2. Planning and Coordination:Organizing a fashion show requires careful planning, from choosing the narrative, choreography to styling the models.
  3. Engage the Audience: These events let people experience fashion up close, making it more exciting and memorable.

    4. Stay updated on recruitment dates and requirements by following the fashion club EUNIOA on social media channels.

Events Conducted by Eunoia

  • Fiesta Showcase, 2024: Demonstrated how today's corporate professionals can bring their full selves to work, expressing their unique style within the coorporate framework.
