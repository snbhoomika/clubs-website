PRAVEG

The Praveg club at UVCE is a dedicated automotive engineering club that focuses on the design, construction, and innovation of racing cars. It provides students with a hands-on experience in the field of motorsports engineering, enabling them to explore various aspects of vehicle design, dynamics, and performance optimization. Here’s a look at what makes Praveg unique:

Key Activities

  1. Design and Simulation: Members start by designing every component of the car digitally, including chassis, suspension, aerodynamics, and engine integration, using advanced CAD tools.
  2. Fabrication and Assembly: Praveg members build the car from scratch, working on everything from welding the frame to assembling the powertrain, installing braking systems, and adding body panels.
  3. Testing and Tuning: Every prototype undergoes rigorous testing, including track testing for speed, handling, and braking.
  4. Preparation for Competitions: Praveg regularly participates in events such as Formula Bharat and SAE BAJA, where they showcase their racing cars and compete with other engineering teams from across the country.
  5. Workshops and Knowledge Sharing: The club conducts workshops on automotive topics, such as vehicle dynamics, engine mechanics, and the latest racing technologies.
