VACHANA
Vachana helps you find your voice and refine skills like debating, stand-up comedy, mimicry, and public speaking. With regular events and seminars, it offers a platform to build confidence, enhance vocabulary, and prepare for interviews and professional settings.

In a democratic society, where words hold power, Vachana provides a supportive space to improve speaking abilities, from delivering impactful speeches to engaging in debates or comedy. Beyond speech, it fosters personal growth, self-assurance, and influence. Vachana is more than a club—it’s a community that empowers you to make a difference through the art of communication.
EVENTS
  • WOLF OF RED STREET
    What's the use of a moon weapon in the hands of a coward? What good is a mirror in the hands of a blind person? What use is a philosopher's silence?
    In celebration of the grand festival of Kannada Rajyotsava, we present a golden platform for thinkers and debaters. 'Wolf of Red Street' is the ultimate competition showcasing the best of Kannada language's literary knowledge, persuasive skills, oratory talents, business acumen, and observational skills.

    Get ready to test your wits by solving puzzles and challenges that will secure your spot in the thrilling crime investigation. This immersive experience promises to be an unforgettable journey into the realm of mystery and investigation, putting your intelligence and problem-solving skills to the ultimate test.

  • Participate Actively: Be ready to engage in events and seminars throughout the year. It's all about growing together and having fun.
  • Commitment to Personal Growth: We value members who are dedicated to their own development and keen on using their voice to make a difference.
  • Positive Contribution: Join us in making the club a lively community by being active and contributing your unique talents and perspectives.
