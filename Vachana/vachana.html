VACHANA

Vachana is all about helping you find your voice. Whether you're into debating, stand-up comedy, bargaining, lecturing, mimicry, or public speaking, this club gives you the perfect stage to refine your skills. In today's world, being able to communicate effectively is crucial, and Vachana knows just how to help. With events and seminars happening throughout the year, you'll get plenty of chances to practice and improve. You'll find yourself growing more confident, enhancing your vocabulary, and becoming better prepared for interviews and professional settings.

Living in a democratic society means our words carry a lot of weight, and Vachana is here to make sure we use them well. The club provides a supportive environment where you can work on your speaking abilities, whether it's delivering powerful speeches, engaging in heated debates, or making people laugh with your comedy routines. The experiences you gain here go beyond just improving your speech—they help you grow as a person, build self-assurance, and prepare you to be an influential voice in society. Vachana is more than just a club; it's a community that values the power of words and helps you harness that power to make a difference.





EVENTS
    WOLF OF RED STREET
    What's the use of a moon weapon in the hands of a coward? What good is a mirror in the hands of a blind person? What use is a philosopher's silence?
    In celebration of the grand festival of Kannada Rajyotsava, we present a golden platform for thinkers and debaters. 'Wolf of Red Street' is the ultimate competition showcasing the best of Kannada language's literary knowledge, persuasive skills, oratory talents, business acumen, and observational skills.









    ಉಳಿದವರು ಕಂಡಂತೆ
    Dive into the world of crime with "ಉಳಿದವರು ಕಂಡಂತೆ"!
    Get ready to test your wits by solving puzzles and challenges that will secure your spot in the thrilling crime investigation. Over the course of two days, you'll unleash your inner Sherlock Holmes, gathering clues, cracking codes, and uncovering the truth. This immersive experience promises to be an unforgettable journey into the realm of mystery and investigation, putting your intelligence and problem-solving skills to the ultimate test.
