Vinimaya

Vinimaya is the pop-culture club of UVCE. It is a nest for all anime, movies, literature, TV series, and music enthusiasts. A cultural club under Adhamya, this club is home to fun, learning, friendship, and growth.

From MUNs to music and movie reviews, from book club meets to writing competitions, from escape rooms to debates, this club does it all! Vinimaya is where creativity meets passion, and fun is always on the agenda!

Participants must be enthusiastic about

  • Pop culture
  • TV
  • Cinema
  • Books
  • MUN
