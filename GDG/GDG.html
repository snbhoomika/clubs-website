GDG

Google Developer Groups are global communities where developers can connect, learn, and share knowledge.

GDGs provide a platform for developers to collaborate on projects, attend workshops and meetups, and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in technology. At GDG on campus UVCE, we are excited to introduce the community spirit to the college and empower budding developers.

Join us to get a chance to explore Google Technologies, build projects, and give back to the community!

Criteria to Join

    Our club consists of three squads,
  • Tech squad: Technical knowledge, Problem-solving skills,collaboration.
  • Design Squad: knowledge of Design tools, Vedio editing skills, Creativity, Team collaboration.
  • Outreach Squad: Good communication skills, Creativity, Proactiveness.

Stay updated on recruitment dates and requirements by following the GDG Club on social media channels.

Events Conducted by GDG

    1. Bit N Build Hackathon: will be an International Level Hackathon which will be collectively organized at various universities around the globe and in various states of India. A grand event conducts at the time of milagro, contains mainly 2 rounds.
    • - Internal Hackathon in various countries Every University will conduct an independent Internal Hackathon for their country.
    • - Internal Hackathon in every state Every University will conduct an independent Internal Hackathon for their state.
    2. Gen AI Study Jams:It provides students an opportunity to kickstart their learning on Gen AI technology, and implement the learning by enabling them to build projects on the Google Cloud console.
