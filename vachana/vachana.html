VACHANA

Vachana helps you find your voice, whether in debating, comedy, public speaking, or mimicry. Offering events and seminars year-round, it boosts confidence, vocabulary, and communication skills. In a supportive environment, members practice using words powerfully—both for personal growth and professional readiness. Vachana is a community that values words and empowers members to make an impact.





EVENTS
    WOLF OF RED STREET
    What's the use of a moon weapon in the hands of a coward? What good is a mirror in the hands of a blind person? What use is a philosopher's silence?
    In celebration of the grand festival of Kannada Rajyotsava, we present a golden platform for thinkers and debaters. 'Wolf of Red Street' is the ultimate competition showcasing the best of Kannada language's literary knowledge, persuasive skills, oratory talents, business acumen, and observational skills.









    ಉಳಿದವರು ಕಂಡಂತೆ
    Dive into the world of crime with "ಉಳಿದವರು ಕಂಡಂತೆ"!
    Get ready to test your wits by solving puzzles and challenges that will secure your spot in the thrilling crime investigation. Over the course of two days, you'll unleash your inner Sherlock Holmes, gathering clues, cracking codes, and uncovering the truth. This immersive experience promises to be an unforgettable journey into the realm of mystery and investigation, putting your intelligence and problem-solving skills to the ultimate test.






WANT TO JOIN ?
ELIGIBILITY
  • Love for Speech Activities: If you enjoy debating, stand-up comedy, bargaining, lecturing, mimicry, or public speaking, this club is a perfect fit for you.

  • Eager to Improve: Are you keen to enhance your speaking skills, build confidence, and expand your vocabulary? This club is designed to help you do just that!

  • Participate Actively: Be ready to engage in events and seminars throughout the year. It's all about growing together and having fun.

  • Commitment to Personal Growth: We value members who are dedicated to their own development and keen on using their voice to make a difference.

  • Positive Contribution: Join us in making the club a lively community by being active and contributing your unique talents and perspectives.
