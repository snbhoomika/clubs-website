TATVA

Tatva is the acting and drama club at University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) in Bangalore. It serves as a platform for students passionate about theater, drama, and acting to hone their skills, express creativity, and engage in the performing arts. Tatva organizes various events, workshops, and performances that allow members to practice acting, explore different theatrical styles, and collaborate with others in UVCE. The club likely participates in intra- and inter-college competitions and provides opportunities for students to develop confidence, teamwork, and stage presence.

Who can be a part of our club ?

  1. Auditions: Tatva typically holds auditions where students can showcase their acting skills. You may be asked to perform a monologue, enact a scene, or participate in an improvisation exercise to demonstrate your talent and versatility.
  2. Passion for Theatre: The club values members who have a genuine interest in theater and a willingness to learn and improve. Showing enthusiasm for acting and drama can make a positive impression.
  3. Team work and Collaborate: Acting in a group setting requires strong teamwork skills. The selection process may include group activities or exercises to assess how well you collaborate with others.

    4. Stay updated on recruitment dates and requirements by following the acting club Tatva on social media channels.

Events Conducted by Tatva.

  • Street plays: Often based on social or cultural themes, performed in open spaces to engage a large audience.
  • Stage Dramas: The club puts on well-rehearsed plays that can range from classic to contemporary stories.
  • Monologues and Short Skits: Solo performances where actors present powerful monologues or short skits.
