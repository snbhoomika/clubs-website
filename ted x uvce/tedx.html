Speakers

Arun Giri

Anchor | Entrepreneur

Arun Giri leads the Employee Experience portfolio at Zee Entertainment, focusing on workplace culture and employee satisfaction. Known for his versatility, Arun is an anchor, trainer, dancer, beatboxer, entrepreneur, employee engagement expert, and life coach. He has hosted over 1800 shows across India and shared the stage with celebrities like Virat Kohli. Originally an engineer, Arun pursued his passion for entertainment and engagement, becoming a TEDx speaker with his talk, "Never Work a Day," where he encourages people to follow their dreams despite obstacles. Arun has inspired many through his motivational speeches at colleges, tackling topics like societal pressures around career choices. He’s also a Corporate Talent Championship titleholder, recognized for his impressive beatboxing. Arun’s dynamic personality and passion continue to inspire others to pursue their passions in life and work.

Dr Pavan Kumar P

Architect Planner

Dr. P. Pavan Kumar, an esteemed Architect Planner, is celebrated for his research on urban poverty and Smart Cities. Currently the Chairman and Head of the Department of Architecture at UVCE, he has transformed the department by introducing postgraduate courses and establishing it as a Research Center for the first time since 1967. With over 21 years of experience, Dr. Kumar is dedicated to fostering high educational standards, managing curriculum development, and inspiring students and professionals. His work focuses on sustainable urban solutions and advancing architecture to meet the evolving challenges of modern cities.

Kriti Aggarwal

Writer

Kriti Aggarwal's TEDx talk celebrates the transformative power of second and third chances, sharing her journey from "Why me?" to "What now?" She emphasizes resilience, personal growth, and the value of embracing challenges as opportunities. Through humor and heartfelt stories, Kriti inspires her audience to rise after setbacks, highlighting that failure is just a step toward success. Her message encourages giving back to the community, using personal experiences to uplift others. Kriti’s story is a powerful reminder of how resilience and a proactive mindset can lead to personal and societal impact.

Naveen Athresh

Innovator

Naveen Athresh, honored as one of Forbes India's top 100 leaders in 2020, exemplifies outstanding leadership and resilience. As the sole leader from Rakuten to receive this recognition, Naveen has successfully led cross-geography product engineering and science teams, especially through the unprecedented challenges of the pandemic. His adaptive leadership, deep understanding of team dynamics, and motivational approach helped his teams stay focused and innovative, contributing significantly to Rakuten’s success. Naveen’s recognition not only highlights his achievements but also his potential to make lasting impacts in product engineering and leadership.

Prakash Belawadi

Actor | Director

Prakash Belawadi, a celebrated actor, director, and theatre personality from Bangalore, Karnataka, has made significant contributions across Bollywood and regional cinemas like Sandalwood and Tollywood. Known for his versatility and powerful performances, he has earned both critical acclaim and a loyal fan base. Beyond the arts, Prakash is a dedicated advocate for social causes, actively supporting environmental protection and the rights of the differently-abled. His commitment to using his platform for societal good makes him an inspiring figure both on and off the screen.

Prof v k Gaur

Geophysicist | Seismologists

Vinod Gaur, an esteemed physicist and honorary emeritus scientist at CSIR Fourth Paradigm Institute in Bangalore, has made impactful contributions to Earth and planetary sciences over decades. His career includes roles as a University Professor, Institute Director, and Government Secretary, earning him numerous awards. Professor Gaur advocates for the interconnectedness of disciplines like Science, Arts, Music, and Culture, seeing them as complementary aspects of human creativity. His holistic approach and dedication to science continue to inspire the scientific community and beyond.

Vivek Kaul

Creative Director

Vivek Kaul, a creative director and strategy advisor, uses his innovative ideas to drive success for various companies and start-ups. A UVCE graduate, he chose a career focused on creativity over conventional paths, earning numerous awards and honors for his impactful contributions. Beyond work, Vivek is dedicated to humanitarian causes, actively supporting Akshaya Patra's mission to provide free meals to underprivileged children. His journey exemplifies how creativity and compassion can make a difference, inspiring young individuals to pursue their passions and contribute meaningfully to society.
