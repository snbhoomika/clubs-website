E-cell

E-Cell UVCE is the Entrepreneurship Cell at University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering, consisting of students of UVCE who are passionate about entrepreneurship, finance, marketing, etc.

Join the vibrant E-Cell UVCE community and unlock your entrepreneurial spirit. Our membership is a gateway to amazing opportunities, designed to fuel your ambitions and set you on the path to success.

E-CELL aims to

  • Foster innovation and entrepreneurial spirit among students.
  • Promote startup culture and entrepreneurship education.
  • Provide resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities.
  • Encourage collaboration, creativity, and innovation.
  • Educate students on all aspects of finance, from businesses and stocks to money management and taxes.

What we do in E-CELL?

  • Participation Certificates: Exclusive participation certificates for every event you attend. Highlight your dedication to learning and growth.
  • Stock Market Workshops and Events: Master Financial Markets!
  • Resume Help : Craft Your Success Story!
  • Startup Visits : Experience Innovation Firsthand!
  • Alumni connections : Your Network is your Net Worth!
  • Exclusive Time with Speakers : Learn from the Best
  • Demat Account Opening : Start Your Investment Journey

Events Conducted by E-CELL

  1. Bull Market: Participants had a 48-hour window to execute virtual trades, using their market knowledge and analytical abilities to maximize profits. The competition fostered a spirit of learning and challenge, as traders navigated real-time stock simulations.
  2. Block Chain Workshop: E-Cell UVCE, in collaboration with IIT-Bombay's innovative start-up SwapSo, held an exclusive workshop on "Exploring Web3 and Blockchain Technologies." This event aimed to demystify blockchain and its applications, drawing a crowd of enthusiastic students and tech lovers.
  3. E-Cell IIT Indore X E-Cell UVCE: E-Cell UVCE has formed a valuable association with E-Cell IIT Indore, marking a significant achievement for our team,including major events like the session with Ishan Sharma.
