To protect the environment and raise awareness of environmental issues.

Youth is the backbone of our nation. A small step from the youth can bring a huge difference in fulfilling the vision of a clean and green India. Working progressively towards this goal, we have our own student run club, go green and clean club,G2C2 spreading every bit of ecological awareness and making a change in this world.

Criteria to Join

Interest in Environmental Issues, Commitment to Activities,Volunteer Experience, some clubs value previous volunteer experience, especially if it's related to environmental work.

Events Conducted by G2C2

The G2C2 Go Green Clean Club at UVCE significantly enriches campus life by its activities. It contributes by:

    1. Dichotomy A grand event conducts at the time of milagro, contains mainly 2 rounds.
    • Deck Detectives: Occasion when detectives becomes PT Usha and Milkha Singh, where detectives should run against time to incover hidden cards across the college.
    • Thesis Thrive: Candidates delved into real-world environmental court cases to not only test their analytical and presentation skills also to emphasize the critical importance of spreading awareness about environmental issues.
  2. Dhoo Jing Jing: A funny event organizes during Jaatre!.
  3. Vaibhava: A event featuring two versions, a festival that combined environment and literature and glamorous that audience's Kannada trend.
  4. Green Warrior Challenge: Be part of the solution, not part of pollution. A monthly based activity, where warriors have to give solutions for the given environmental related challenges.
  5. Eco-Spooky Halloween: A fun and refreshing activity where halloween is at the top point for consideration.
