IEEE UVCE

striding Ahead

Description

  • IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.
  • IEEE UVCE is student branch of IEEE in UVCE.
  • The committee aims at instilling the spirit of innovation, creativity and collaboration.

Events-Conducted

1. Kagada

An annual national-level technical student conference.

It includes: Paper Presentation, Poster Presentation, Project Presentation tracks

Aims to encourage research and innovation at the undergraduate level.

Humanitarian activities such as "Ottige Kaliyona" for teaching government school students and "Food for Cause" a charity food stall, are also part of this event.

2. Codefury

It is an annual national-level hackathon organized by the IEEE UVCE Computer Society.

The event is designed to foster innovation and creativity in coding among participants from various colleges.

It features multiple tracks, primarily focusing on web and app development.

The event typically involves participants creating a web or mobile app based on a given problem statement, which they then present to a panel of judges.

CodeFury is highly regarded for its organization and the opportunities it provides for young developers to demonstrate and hone their coding skills.

3. Impetus

IEEE UVCE organizes a national-level technical fest called IMPETUS, which has been held annually since 2001. This event serves as a significant platform for technical enthusiasts to showcase their skills and innovations. IMPETUS features a variety of activities, including flagship events, general competitions, and gaming arenas.

Contribution

  • The organiztion's commitment to enhancing and upgrading the core knowledge base of its members to the industry standards is reflected in the host of activities organized by it throughout the year.
  • The calendar is marked by Kagada- The National Level Technical conference Impetus - the Technical Fest and is filled with ample amount of workshops, symposiums, guest lectures and Industrial visits.
