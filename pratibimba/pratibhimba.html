About Us

Discover your creative spark with Prathibimba, the Fine Art and Photography Club at UVCE. Whether you're a passionate artist or an enthusiastic photographer, Prathibimba offers you a canvas to paint your imagination and a lens to capture the world through your unique perspective. This is your space to explore new techniques, learn from peers, and get inspired by the creativity around you.

At Prathibimba, creativity knows no bounds. You'll have the opportunity to participate in workshops, exhibitions, and collaborative projects that will push your artistic limits and broaden your horizons. Meet like-minded individuals who share your love for art and photography, and together, create something truly beautiful.
