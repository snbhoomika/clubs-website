About Us

Founded in 2022, Prathibimba is more than just a club; it’s a vibrant community for photographers, artists, and creatives driven by the passion to explore, express, and inspire. Here, creativity knows no boundaries. Through Prathibimba, we create a unique space where the art of storytelling meets the skill of capturing life's moments, and where each member’s vision is celebrated.
